Draft Lottery Rules

  • The bottom 9 teams in the regular season qualify for the draft lottery (The number of teams eligible is based on the percentage of total NHL teams eligible for the NHL draft lottery).
  • All eligible teams qualify for the 1st overall selection.
  • No team can worsen their draft position by more than one spot.
  • Odds of improving one's draft position are based upon final regular season positioning. Odds of obtaining the 1st overall selection are as follows:
    • 20th - 35%
    • 19th - 22%
    • 18th - 13%
    • 17th - 9%
    • 16th - 7%
    • 15th - 5%
    • 14th - 4%
    • 13th - 3%
    • 12th - 2%
